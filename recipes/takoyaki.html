Takoyaki

Description

Takoyaki, or Grilled Octopus Balls, are one of Japan's best-known street food that
originated in Osaka.Whether you make a traditional style with bits of octopus or other
alternatives, these ball-shaped dumplings are fun to make with your friends and family!
