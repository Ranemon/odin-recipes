Yakisoba

Description

Yakisoba is a classic Japanese stir-fried noodle dish that's seasoned with a sweet and savory sauce similar to Worcestershire sauce. Use any proteins you like: pork, chicken, shrimp, or calamari. For vegetarians, just swap it with tofu or shiitake mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • ½ onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 3 shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 green onions
  • 4 cabbage leaves
  • 340g sliced pork belly
  • 2Tbsp natural-flavored oil
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 servings yakisoba noodles
  • 4-6Tbsp Yakisoba Sauce

For the Yakisoba Sauce (Makes ½ cup, 120 ml)

  • 4Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 4tsp oyster sauce
  • 4tsp ketchup
  • 2tsp soy sauce
  • 2-4tsp sugar

For the Toppings(Optional)

  • aonori
  • pickled red ginger
